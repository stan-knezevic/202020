What is the 20-20-20 Rule?

Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away from you for 20 seconds.
This rule of thumb is just a simple mnemonic device that reminds you to give your eyes a break from continuous screen time.
Although continous screen time may not cause permanent eye damage, eye strain from dry eyes and decreased blinking can cause discomfort.
